About Agneya Vishwasanthi Foundation

Guided by Sanatana Dharma, we nurture inner transformation and collective harmony.

Our Mission

Spread the eternal wisdom of Sanatana Dharma and create a world rooted in peace, compassion, and spiritual awakening.

Our Vision

A harmonious society aligned with Dharma, balancing inner growth and service to humanity.

Our Journey

Foundation

Seeds of Vishwasanthi sown with the vision of sharing spiritual knowledge and collective service.

Community Building

Meditation camps, seva activities, and satsangs shaping a community of seekers.

Expansion

Programs reached more people with teachings of Sanatana Dharma and humanitarian service.

Today

Guiding individuals and communities toward peace, dharma, and spiritual awakening.

Be Part of the Journey

Join hands with us in spreading the light of Sanatana Dharma and creating a better world.
